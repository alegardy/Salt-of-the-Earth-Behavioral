About Us

Salt of the Earth Behavioral facilitates behavioral management for individuals with developmental challenges. We are a God-centered clinic devoted to compassionate care rooted in strong values and morals. Our mission is to uplift individuals and families while serving our community with integrity, kindness, reliability, and honesty.

Goals & Objectives

Our goal is to empower individuals to develop social and communication skills that help them navigate everyday life. We celebrate individuality and help clients embrace their uniqueness while gaining tools to thrive.

Our Story

Founded by two Mexican American women with a passion for behavioral health, Salt of the Earth Behavioral was built on compassion and community. With experience in Applied Behavioral Analysis and education from California Baptist University, the company blends clinical expertise with faith-based values to provide meaningful care.
